## üïπ Game Overview
In **Box World**, you navigate a seamless grid-based world where your progress is automatically synchronized to your browser. Hunt boxes, survive boss encounters, and manage your resources at the Merchant's Shop.

### **Current Features**
*   **1,000+ Zone Engine**: A high-performance region-based world system that allows for massive map expansion with zero lag. Currently only 4 region and 8 zone.
*   **Persistent Progress**: Automatic `localStorage` synchronization for Score, High Score, HP, and World Position.
*   **Dynamic Combat**: Battle enemies and bosses with real-time health regeneration and collision physics.
*   **Merchant System**: Use your hard-earned Score as currency to restore health.
*   **Mobile-First Design**: Custom-built virtual joystick optimized for touch-screen responsiveness.
